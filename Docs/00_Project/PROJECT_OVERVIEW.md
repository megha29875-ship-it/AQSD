# Executive Summary

AQSD is a modular, AI-assisted market analytics and decision support platform designed for analysing the Indian equity and derivatives markets.

The platform combines market structure analysis, option analytics, historical data, and explainable AI to produce structured, evidence-based market assessments.

AQSD is designed as a **decision support platform**, not an automated trading system. Its purpose is to help traders and investors understand market conditions through transparent analytics and well-defined reasoning.

---

# Vision

To build an institutional-grade platform that transforms raw market data into actionable market intelligence through analytics, historical evidence, and explainable AI.

# Architecture Philosophy

AQSD is built using independent modules.

Each engine performs a single responsibility and produces structured output.

Higher-level engines combine those outputs into increasingly comprehensive assessments.

This layered approach improves maintainability, testing, and explainability.

---

# Long-Term Roadmap

The platform will evolve through three stages.

## Stage 1 – Market Analytics

Collect, process, and analyse live market data.

## Stage 2 – Market Intelligence

Combine analytics with historical behaviour and verified trading knowledge.

## Stage 3 – Institutional Decision Support

Deliver explainable, evidence-based insights for professional market participants.
